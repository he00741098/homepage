Jesse He - Electrical Engineering Student at UIUC

Jesse He is a 19-year-old student from Oregon currently pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). With a deep interest in computer hardware, networking, and software development, Jesse's portfolio at jessehe.net showcases projects ranging from personal server configurations to multiplayer game development.

Technically, Jesse specializes in languages like Rust, JavaScript, and Java. He manages a personal homelab featuring a Supermicro server running Proxmox to host various services and optimize network performance. Current projects include the development of a multiplayer version of the Chrome Dino game (dino.sweep.rs) and a multiplayer Minesweeper game for Steam, leveraging a high-performance backend written in Rust.

Beyond engineering, Jesse has a background in music, having studied piano and clarinet throughout high school. As a student at UIUC, he is focused on the intersection of hardware systems and systems programming.
